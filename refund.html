1. Eligibility for Refund

  • Refunds are applicable only for payments made towards salad subscriptions, premium meal plans, or other paid features.
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days from the original payment date.
  • Customers must provide a valid reason along with order details to initiate a refund request.

2. Non-Refundable Items

  • One-time service fees or administrative charges.
  • Payments made under limited-time promotions or discounted offers.
  • Orders that have already been processed, prepared, or delivered.

3. Refund Process

  • To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@saladshop.in.
  • Once approved, refunds will be processed within 10 business days.
  • All refunds will be credited back to the original payment method used.

4. Changes to This Policy

Salad Shop reserves the right to update or revise this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
