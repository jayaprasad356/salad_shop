1. Eligibility for Refund

  • Refunds are applicable only for payments made towards salad subscriptions, premium meal plans, or other paid features.
  • Requests for refunds must be made within 7 days of the original payment date.
  • Customers must provide valid reasons and order details when requesting a refund.

2. Non-Refundable Items

  • One-time service fees and administrative charges.
  • Payments made under special promotions or discounted offers.
  • Orders that have already been processed, prepared, or delivered.

3. Refund Process

  • To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@saladshop.in.
  • If approved, refunds will be processed within 10 business days.
  • The refunded amount will be credited back to the original payment method.

4. Changes to Refund Policy

Salad Shop reserves the right to update or modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
